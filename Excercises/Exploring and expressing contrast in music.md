## Exploring and expressing contrast in music

Exercise - Exploring and expressing contrast in music

> [!Example] Song 
> Momofuku

1. Play the song, let them dance freely, get to know the song
2. Go through the song together, say what are the differences in each part
3. Limit the dance and trying to express the contrast in the limit (after they dance each option, show them our example)
	1. Staying in close embrace, no body shapes, no headmovements, only STEPS
	2. Staying in close embrace with body shapes
	3. Open embrace with simple elements
	4. Only headmovement (for advanced)
	5. Only body shapes (chest + hips)
4. Combine it in their own way
