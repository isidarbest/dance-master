## Exercise - What feels comfortable for your body

**Everytime go one type of exercise on all the songs**

Songs: 
[[Birdi - Skinny love]]
[[Sariel Orenda, DeviRa, Mose - Azul]]
[[Pabllo Vittar - K.O.]]
[[Sage the Gemini - Watchachacha]]

### Solo
1) with closed yes on the spot
	*explore the movement of your body, what feels comfortable for you and what you feel in the music*
2) you can play only with your upper half of body (from hips up)
3) only the bottom part of your body (including hips)
4) only steps
5) combine everything, choose which song tells us what

### Couples
Trying to do the same, switching also roles. *Don't think about leading so much. Relax your frame and let anything happen. Be aware of your partner. *
