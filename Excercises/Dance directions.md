# Exercise - Dance directions
## One directional dance

**Task**: You need to get from one side of the dance hall to the other

Song: [[Faylasuf - Romance]]

First tell them the task, let them try, then show them how we would do it.

1) you have three 8 counts
2) you can do only specific figures

	a) 1 basic (there and back or continue), open to lunch + turn, viragina
	b) 1 basic, open to lateral + 1 lateral, 1 in and out + turn
	c) 2 basic, lunch + three turns

>Adjust based on the size of the space

## Unusual shapes

One couple will dance and the rest will create obstacles, the couple need to shape his dance to fill the space, obstacles will move a little bit and will react in small movement on the couple

Can be done in several groups
