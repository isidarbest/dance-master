## Leaders leading only with their body
Good position to exercise at: 
Leader is in position of [[Ohrádka]] (arms wrapped around partner, perpendicular), tries to lead for follower either step forward or back only with torsion of his body.

*We can do before:* 
Leaders on the spot, F staying in open lateral position. L is leading her steps around her in circle only with rotation of his body. 

Leader staying on the spot, with feet together. Leading only with their chest rotation.
