## How follower can show boundaries 
From softest to most firm 
1. Adjusting steps 
2. Adding tension in her frame / body 
3. Placing a physical boundary on leaders body (stiffen the frame, put her hand on the chest of the leader when he wants to take her closer)
4. Saying out loud what she feels and what could make it feel better
