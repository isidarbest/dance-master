## Catch and release basic
1) We train only 
   L are going two steps back and hugging F with left hand, releasing the right hand
2) Focusing on making the spring with our legs, lowering in our knees, being more soft
3) Trying to release our partner by coming back to front leg and turning our chest to neutral (don't add any extra energy)
4) show the steps of turn to followers
5) show work of hands to followers
6) show leaders how to change their hand on back of F to get back to basic
