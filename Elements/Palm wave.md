## Explanation Palm
Imagine like someone attaches a string to your lowest rib and pulls it towards the ceiling. The rest of the body is quite relaxed.

We can imagine our body like separate parts
- head
- shoulders
- rib cage

Each of the parts will get in the finish line (line above our standing leg) in a different time. First it will be our rib cage, then shoulder and then the head. 
- In the [[Snake wave]] it is the opposite
