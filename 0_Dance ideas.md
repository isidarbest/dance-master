### Room altars 
- [[2025-10-19]] from [[2025-10 Cedric and Romane Immersion]]

		- steps, headmovements, hips, bodymovements
		- the closer we are to some point, the more we have to express with this tool. If we are in the middle, we express with everything.

# Exercises
*Idea from [[2025-09 Zouktime Croatia]] when dancing with [[Romane Leterme]]*: 
Cvičení na headmovementy - pomoci partnerce napnout ruce a celé tělo co nejvíc nahoru a potom pustit. Jakmile pustíme, tak uvolnit napěti do headmovement pozice. Je možné zkoušet pozici hlavy vzadu nebo na boku. Důležité je, aby partnerka nezkolabovala, držela si vlastní tělo, ale zároveň se snažila uvolnit a povolit energii projít dolů. Dá se potom napojit na možnost tohle navést - jdeme z napnuté pozice, ale v uzavřeném hrudníku. 

Playing with the position of "high five" in basic step. With proper elasticity we can lead many things. 
Things that are neccessary for this: 
- knowing how to react with your frame on recieved pressure (returning it back) even when it increases during some step

When leaders are leading F back, they create the movement more with their legs than frame, the frame is just releasing the tension as a spring.

Options: 
- turn of the follower to the left and right, lead with hand
- turn of the follower with left or right inwards or outwards with pushing unequally to one side
- changing the direction ([[Převedení]]), can be also added the inward turn (I saw it teaching [[Michal Chromčák]] in lamba)
- building pressure and then releasing into calmness (back step for follower)
### Exercise for musicality
We set up some parts of songs that leaders stop at. Then the lady says what she feels there in the music. The leader has to do it. Then we change ladies and repeat just with another part of the song. The leader can dance whatever in between. 
Can be connected with topic planning the musicality emphasis

### Explanation for how we express music
Connect the way we can sing rhythm of one word in song to way we express musicality.

### Idea from dancing with Julia Utig. 
Trying to play with the limit positions. Always somehow get your partner into disbalance and going into the biggest limit (with a lot of control and slowly) and then create something out of it. Trying to lose the fear of imbalance. 

### Exercise for Musicality
People are dancing on some song and I am stopping the music randomly. When I stop they have to stop at the last step and make the movement super Slow motion so they never stop moving.
### Communicating the inspiration
In couples, one couple is dancing, second one is looking, after minute or so, they tell to the couple who was dancing, what they are doing and how they are dancing (what is inspiring for them or what they like). Try to be observing, not judging. 
### Taking inspiration during dancing from other leader
- splitting them into two couples
- one couple makes one 8 (or 2 if it makes sence like boomerang) and stops, the second couple has to repeat their movement
- once the second couple is finished, the first one can go again
- first start without music or on some lyrical song
