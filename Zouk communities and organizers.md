Zpráva od Han Han:
In Hanoi city in Vietnam they have two zouk annual events, one is Brazouky Vietnam by Phuong and Trang of PT Zouk. And the other one is Heart Zouk Marathon by Yen of Heart Zouk Dance. 

I’d recommend to contact Yen, as her event might be more aligned with you. 

You’ll love the local community in Hanoi Vietnam. Warm and welcoming. During these two Hanoi zouk events various dancers from South East Asia they all come together.
